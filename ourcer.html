Awards and Certificates

Piel color received the ECO prize from the Federation of Egyptian Industries FEI and the Environmental compliance Office ECO and gained the prize of Pioneering Works For Sustaining Development in the last two editions (2017 & 2018). Since we are committed to the criteria of cleaner production and sustainability, Piel Color was awarded the ISO 9001 in environmental sustainability, ISO 14001 in quality, ISO 18001 in health and Occupational safety procedures as well as ISO 26000 in social responsibility. In the meantime, we are working on ISO 50001 for energy efficiency (EnMS) through a cooperation program with UNIDO. Piel Color also finished the audit of the LWG certificate from the Leather World Group and also the certificate of PEF (Product Environmental Footprint) from UNIDO. Moreover

Piel Color have been awarded many certificate and prizes that assure its entity and the consideration it gained:

  • ✓ ISO 9001 in environmental sustainability.
  • ✓ ISO 14001 in quality.
  • ✓ ISO 18001 in Occupational safety and health procedures.
  • ✓ ISO 26000 in social responsibility.
  • ✓ LWG certificate from Leather Working Group.
  • ✓ PEF certificate from UNIDO as a pioneer Egyptian program (Product Environmental Footprint).
  • ✓ The ECO prize for 2 years (2017 & 2018) from the Federation of Egyptian Industries (FEI) and the Environmental compliance Office ECO (PIONEERING WORKS FOR SUSTAINING DEVELOPMENT), since we are committed to the criteria of the cleaner production.
