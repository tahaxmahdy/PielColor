WHO WE ARE?

Piel Color Company for Tanning and Finish Leather was established in 1945 Located in Qwesina , Menoufia , Egypt Piel Color group is considered a landmark and pioneering company in the tanning and leather industries in Egypt and the Middle East. A Testified Quality of Crust Leather, Finished Leather, Industrial Gelatin, Amino Acid, Dog Chew, and Biodiesel. According to a long experience since 1945 Piel Color moved to a new horizon in this industry through a partnership with the most famous international specialized companies from Spain, Italy, and Germany.

Our Mission

  • Our mission is to set the benchmark for excellence in the leather industry by delivering the highest quality crust and finished leather products, positioning us as a premium choice within the market. Combining Egyptian craftsmanship with Spanish expertise, we pride ourselves on producing innovative leather and by-products that not only meet but exceed the expectations of discerning global clients.
  • With the largest market share in the region, we are committed to sustainable practices and ethical sourcing, offering products that justify their premium price through unmatched quality and durability. We strive to expand our reach and reinforce our reputation in elite markets worldwide, supporting the growth of leather industries in Egypt and the Middle East with a focus on integrity, innovation, and social responsibility.

Our Vision

  • We aspire to be a leading global provider of premium leather solutions, renowned for our expertise in crafting both crust and finished leather products. By blending Egyptian craftsmanship with Spanish innovation, we aim to deliver high-quality, sustainable materials that inspire creativity in fashion, upholstery, and beyond. Our commitment extends to pioneering advancements in leather by-products, including gelatin and amino acids, aligning with eco-conscious practices and elevating industry standards.
  • To continue pioneering leather industries in Egypt and the Middle East, we are dedicated to diversifying our products to enhance integration and compete strongly in elite markets worldwide while upholding our values of sustainability and social responsibility. Together, we strive to enhance the value of natural leather and redefine excellence in every piece we produce.
