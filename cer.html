Awards and Certificates

Piel Color have been awarded many certificate and prizes that assure its entity and the consideration it gained:

  • ISO 9001 in environmental sustainability.
  • ISO 14001 in quality.
  • ISO 18001 in Occupational safety and health procedures.
  • ISO 26000 in social responsibility.
  • LWG certificate from Leather Working Group.
  • PEF certificate from UNIDO as a pioneer Egyptian program (Product Environmental Footprint).
  • The ECO prize for 2 years (2017 & 2018) from the Federation of Egyptian Industries (FEI) and the Environmental Compliance Office ECO (PIONEERING WORKS FOR SUSTAINING DEVELOPMENT), since we are committed to the criteria of cleaner production.
