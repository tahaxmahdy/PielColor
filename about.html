WHO WE ARE?

  • Piel Color is one of the leading innovative tanneries in the Middle East.
  • Piel Color Company for Tanning and Finished Leather was established in 1945. Located in Qwesina , Menoufia, Egypt, Piel Color Group is a landmark and pioneering company in the tanning and leather industries across Egypt and the Middle East. It provides a certified quality range of crust leather, finished leather, industrial gelatin, amino acids, dog chews, and biodiesel. Building on extensive experience since 1945, Piel Color has expanded through partnerships with renowned international companies from Spain, Italy, and Germany.

  • 1997

    Piel Color Established

    The company was established in 1987 with the aim of providing innovative solutions in the leather industry.
    It started working in the fields of leather tanning and manufacturing leather products such as dried leather and raw leather (crast).

  • 1997-2000

    Expansion and development

    In the early 1990s, the company expanded its production capacity by adding new production lines and improving tanning techniques.
    A range of products such as finishing leather and artificial leather were developed.

  • 2000-2010

    Technological development

    The company has applied modern technology in the leather tanning process, which has helped improve the quality of the products and increase their ability to meet international market standards.
    Piel Color has begun to expand its export of its products to new markets.

  • 2010 : Present

    Innovation and expansion of products

    In the last decade, the company has developed a range of diversified products such as amino acids and industrial gelatin, as well as finishing leathers and crusts.
    Piel Color has focused on improving its production capabilities and environmental sustainability in industrial processes.
